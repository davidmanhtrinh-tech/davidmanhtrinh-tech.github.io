Erokia

Ambient Wave 56 - (MSfxP7 - 78)

Ambient Wave 56 is part of Erokia’s atmospheric sounds collection, blending stretched textures, layered ambience, and drifting tones to create a reflective listening experience.

The sound design focuses on calmness, introspection, nostalgia, and emotional stillness - matching the dreamlike themes of this media player project. Ambient music is designed less as a performance and more as an atmosphere.

This project explores relaxation through visual ambience, atmospheric audio, and slow interaction design.
